Research Interests

I focus on interdisciplinary researches at medical image analysis and artificial intelligence, for the sake of smart diagnosis and prognosis and individualized therapy planning and tracking. My research draws from the following different domains.

  • Medical image analysis: anatomical structure segmentation, labeling and quantification, lesion detection.

  • Machine learning: deep neural networks, geometric deep learning, graph neural networks.

  • Topological data analysis: computation and learning with topological structures (e.g. vascular).
